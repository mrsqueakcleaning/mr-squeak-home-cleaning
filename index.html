Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ
1 How does Mr. Squeak work?
Mr. Squeak is all about spotless spaces and peace of mind! We use eco-friendly cleaning products and EPA-approved disinfectants to keep your home clean, safe, and healthy. Our cleaners are full-time employees—not temporary hires or contractors—so they’re trained, insured, and bonded to meet Mr. Squeak’s high standards. Plus, booking is easy! Our 100% cashless system lets you schedule, manage, and provide feedback on all your cleanings online.
2 Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
That’s entirely up to you! When booking, you’ll be able to leave entry instructions. If you have a doorman or a safe spot to leave a key, you don’t need to be there. Prefer to stay while we clean? No problem—feel free to stay and go over our checklist with us if you’d like! However, please remember your scheduled appointment. We’ll send you a reminder, but if we can’t access your home due to a lock-out, a fee may apply.
3 Is Mr. Squeak insured and bonded?
Absolutely! We take the trust you place in us seriously. Our team is fully insured and bonded, so you can feel confident about letting us into your home. If anything is accidentally damaged during a cleaning, please contact us within 48 hours, and we’ll make it right.
4 Is Mr. Squeak pet-friendly?
We love pets! But we also understand that some furry friends may feel anxious around new people. If you think your pet might be uncomfortable during a cleaning, consider making temporary arrangements while we’re there. During booking, we’ll ask about your pets to make sure everything goes smoothly.
5 What is the Squeak Guarantee™?
If it's not up to Squeak, we'll clean it again for free! If you're not satisfied with your cleaning, you can request a complimentary re-clean within 48 hours. Some exclusions apply. Your happiness is our priority!
