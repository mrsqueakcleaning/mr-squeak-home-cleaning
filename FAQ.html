Frequently Asked Questions

Book Now Cleaning Checklists

Find Your Answer

1 How Does Mr. Squeak Work?
Booking is simple! Schedule your cleaning online, call us at 844-833-5353, or email contact@mrsqueak.com.
2 Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not at all! You’re welcome to be there, but if not, we can arrange secure access in advance.
3 Is Mr. Squeak insured and bonded?
Yes! We’re fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind.
4 Are your cleaning products safe for pets?
Absolutely! We use pet-friendly cleaning products, and our team is trained to work around pets safely.
5 Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
No, we bring all necessary supplies for a thorough clean. However, if you prefer us to use your own products, let us know when booking. What if I don’t have a mop, bucket, or vacuum?
No worries! Our team arrives fully equipped with professional-grade tools.
- **For Deep Cleaning and Move-In/Move-Out Cleaning:** We bring everything, including a vacuum.
- **For Squeak & Quick™ and Standard Cleaning:** We kindly ask you to provide a vacuum if needed.
6 What if I recently completed a renovation?
For post-renovation cleaning, book through Mr. Squeak Commercial Cleaning.
7 Can I provide instructions or special requests?
Of course! We customize our services based on your needs. Just inform us when booking.
8 Do you clean during or after business hours?
Yes! We offer flexible scheduling, including after-hours cleaning.
9 What is the Squeak Guarantee™?
If your clean isn’t up to standards, we’ll make it right—at no extra cost! Request a re-clean within 48 hours.
10 How do I tip my cleaner?
Tip in cash or through our online system.
11 When does my card get charged?
Payment is processed on the day of service. We accept online payments only—no cash.
12 What is your cancellation policy?
Cancel or reschedule 24+ hours in advance—free. Late cancellations will incur a $50 fee.
13 How do I get a quote for office cleaning?
Request a quote by emailing contact@mrsqueak.com, calling 844-833-5353, or booking online at Mr. Squeak Commercial Cleaning Instant Booking.
